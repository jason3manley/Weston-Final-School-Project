Welcome to Weston Hotels and Resorts

Weston Hotels and resorts are located amidst the rolling hills and tranquil lakes. The village overlooks the beautiful Lake Louisa, which connects 11 lakes and is one of the best place for fishing. You can choose from villas, which can accommodate up to six people. These cozy rentals feature fully equipped kitchens, screened porches as well as stunning views.

Local Attractions

Florida is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. With visitors flocking to various beaches and theme parks for fun, and thrills.

Man-made attractions include Walt Disney World, Busch Gardens and Sea World.

The state also has many natural treasures, more than 8,000 miles of coastline, countless bays, islands, winding waterways, freshwater lakes, hills and forests.

Florida, is known for the Everglades - the sun, sand, and surf that make up Florida’s 1500 kilometers of beaches; the Florida Keys; South Beach, the trendiest place in the world.
